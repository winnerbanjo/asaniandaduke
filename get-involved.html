Donation & Partnerships

The Foundation welcomes partnerships and donations from individuals, organizations, development agencies, and corporate bodies interested in supporting youth and women empowerment, skills development, and cultural sustainability.

Your support directly funds training materials (dyes, fabrics, starch), workshop locations, health checkups for local artisans, and exhibitions that connect artisans with global buyers.
