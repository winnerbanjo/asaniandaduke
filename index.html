Who We Are

Driven by Purpose, Guided by Heritage

Àṣàní and Àdùkẹ́ Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to empowering communities and preserving Nigeria's rich textile crafts.

Our Mission

To empower youths and women through skills development, entrepreneurship, cultural preservation, and sustainable economic opportunities that improve livelihoods and strengthen communities.

Our Vision

To become a leading platform for transforming lives by equipping individuals with practical skills, promoting indigenous creativity, preserving African cultural heritage, and fostering inclusive economic growth.

What We Do

Our Programs & Focus Areas

We offer a wide array of programs tailored to support underserved communities, artisans, and women seeking financial self-reliance.

Àdìrẹ Value-Addition

Comprehensive training in traditional fabric dyeing methods, design pattern creations, and value-addition techniques to make indigenous crafts globally marketable.

Business Workshops

Entrepreneurship, financial literacy, and export readiness workshops hosted in collaboration with leading agencies SMEDAN, ITF, and NEPC.

Youth Empowerment

Providing young people with employable vocational skills, tech-empowerment access, and mentorship directories to start and grow self-sustaining careers.
